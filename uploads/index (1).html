02Our Story

Four generations.
One recipe.

In 1915, Juan Moré opened a small bakery on 15th Street in Ybor City. The name — La Segunda — meant "the second." More than a century later, we are the first name in authentic Cuban bread.

Every loaf still begins before sunrise. High-gluten spring wheat flour milled for us, a slow fermentation, and the signature palmetto leaf laid down the center — the same method Juan used on opening day.

What's changed is the scale. Today La Segunda produces over twenty thousand loaves a day, distributed by Sysco, US Foods, Cheney Brothers and Performance Food Group to every corner of the Southeast and beyond. Restaurant owners, grocery chains and hospitality groups call us for one reason — authenticity doesn't come from a factory line.

It comes from a family that's been doing the same thing, the same way, since 1915.
