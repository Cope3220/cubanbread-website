La Segunda · Motion Addendum A.01 · Motion Language

The Intent

Quiet motion.
Long dwells. One beat.

Motion is not the protagonist of this site — photography is. Every clip is built to live inside the same dark-editorial frame as the stills: it loads on top of an ink background, opens with a slow gesture, holds, and resolves. No whip-pans. No music-video cuts. No drone. If a still won't do, motion earns its place — otherwise we don't shoot it.

Three modes, mirroring the photo brief. Hero motion (one silent loop on the homepage). Process motion (palmetto leaf, proofing trays, hearth — the soul-of-the-brand triplet rendered as living frames). Portrait motion (Anthony and Giuseppe — small editorial b-roll captured during the same afternoon as the stills).

Cuts are slow. Hold each frame past the photographer's instinct — give the viewer time to read crumb, leaf, breath. The shortest clip is 4 seconds. The longest, the dawn dock, is 20. Most live between 6 and 10.

No talking heads on page one. Interview audio, if captured, lives on Our Story as an optional vignette — never on Home, never on Products, never autoplaying with sound anywhere on the site.

Reference Vocabulary

Apple "Shot on iPhone" — restraint, single subject, long dwell, designed silence.

Chef's Table — Vermeer kitchen light, slow push-ins, dignified workflow.

Aesop product films — macro hands, near-zero camera, gestures as narrative.

Eater b-roll grammar — real heat, real motion blur, documentary respect for the room.

Camera Discipline

  • Movement Locked-off or slow slider. No handheld unless the dock.
  • Dwell Begin and end on stillness. Don't cut on motion.
  • Cut Count Most clips are single-take. Loops are seamless.
  • Lens 35–85mm primes for process; 100mm macro for details.
  • Drone / Crane None.

Spec & Sound

4K capture.
LUT to brand.
Silence by default.

All clips share the photo brief's color science — same LUT, same crust formula, same shadow toe. The motion side adds three concerns photography doesn't: frame rate, audio, and the autoplay contract with the visitor.

Capture Specs

  • Resolution 4K UHD (3840 × 2160) minimum. 6K preferred where lens/body allows for reframe and 9:16 derivation.
  • Codec ProRes 422 HQ or 10-bit log internal. No long-GOP master.
  • Base FPS 24p for narrative loops; 30p for dock and timelapse pieces.
  • Slow Motion 60–120 fps for macro process beats (leaf, eggwash, crumb tear). Conformed in post.
  • Color Profile Camera-native log → graded to the 2019 Identity Guide palette via supplied LUT. Match the stills.
  • Audio Capture clean ambient on every roll. 48kHz / 24-bit. Boom + lav at the portrait session.

Grading & Mix Notes

  • Crust Formula Midtone #9D6B3F; speculars to #E5D660. Same target as stills.
  • Shadow Toe Lift to #1a0e08 on hearth/process pieces. Pure #0d0d0d on hero loop only.
  • Highlight Roll-off Soft. Never clip eggwash, never crush red-Cuban paper.
  • Skin Honey-warm midtones on both portraits. Never pink. Match Anthony and Giuseppe across cuts.
  • Sound Bed Diegetic only. Oven crackle, dough thud, paper rustle, the room's room tone. No music score.
  • Web Default Autoplay, muted, looped, playsinline. Sound is a deliberate user action.

Aspect ratios — one capture, four crops

Frame for 16:9 · derive verticals in edit
16:9 Web hero · Process strip · YouTube · Site master
9:16 Reels · Stories · TikTok · Vertical cutdowns
1:1 Feed posts · Inline product loops · Email header
4:5 Portrait social · Newsletter spotlight · LinkedIn

Frame for 16:9, protect the center two-thirds. Vertical and square crops are derived in the edit, never re-shot. Action and faces must live inside the 9:16 safe column so a single take serves four endings.

The Schedule

Three sessions. One extra day.

Every motion piece is captured during a photo session already on the call sheet. The only meaningful cost is one additional day of crew time plus post for the ident and map.

01.

Bakery overnight / morning — motion overlay

Same shoot as Photo Pairing 03 · ~+0.5 day · video unit

Eight of the thirteen pieces. Motion crew shoots in parallel with the photographer — separate camera, separate operator, shared lighting where possible. The hero loop (M01) and process triptych (M02–M04) overlap exactly with photo Shots 01, 20–22. Detail macros (M08–M11) fit in the 15-minute windows between big photo setups. The longest single shooting block of the project.

Clips: M01 · M02 · M03 · M04 · M08 · M09 · M10 · M11  ·  Pairs w/ photo: 01 · 20 · 21 · 22

02.

Modern portraits — same afternoon

Same shoot as Photo Pairing 02 · ~+0 day · second body, same crew

Anthony and Giuseppe filmed during the matched portrait session. A second camera body locked off ¾ at the photo distance, capturing 8-second takes between still frames. No additional lighting, no separate call. The motion must feel like the photograph began to move — same lens, same hour, same light.

Clips: M05 · M06  ·  Pairs w/ photo: 19 · 41

03.

Pre-dawn loading dock — dedicated call

Same dawn as Photo Shot 37 · ~+0.5 day · sync sound required

The one piece that needs a sound recordist on top of the photo unit. Three-shot sequence built into a single 20-second clip. Coordinate with route dispatch — we need a real morning load, not staged. Photo Shot 37 captured between motion takes 2 and 3, same talent, same vehicle.

Clip: M07  ·  Pairs w/ photo: 37  ·  Add: boom op + recordist

04.

Post only · ident & map mograph

No shoot day · ~2 days motion-design post · vector

Wordmark ident and Tampa Bay route map produced entirely in the edit. Hand off the route data and the brand serif files; deliver master loops in MP4 + ProRes + transparent WebM for in-page overlay. Aligns to the rest of the package in cadence and palette, not in shoot-day terms.

Pieces: M12 (ident) · M13 (map)  ·  Vendor: motion-design partner of record

Delivery — for the editor

Masters ProRes 422 HQ · 4K UHD · 24p (process/portraits/hero) or 30p (dock/ident). Slow-mo conformed. Web H.265 .mp4 at 3840/1920/1280 + WebM fallback. Social 9:16 · 1:1 · 4:5 derived in edit. Naming laseg_MNN_subject_ratio_v01.ext. Autoplay muted, looped, playsinline, poster-framed; sound on user click; reduced-motion disables.
