02Our Story

Four generations.
One recipe.

In 1915, Juan Moré opened a small bakery on 15th Street in Ybor City. The name — La Segunda — meant "the second." More than a century later, La Segunda is a defining name in Tampa-style Cuban bread and a wholesale Cuban bread supplier rooted in Ybor City.

Every loaf still begins before sunrise. High-gluten spring wheat flour milled for us, a slow fermentation, and the signature palmetto leaf laid down the center — the same method Juan used on opening day. La Segunda has baked Cuban bread in Ybor City since 1915.

What's changed is the scale. Today La Segunda produces over 20,000 loaves a day. La Segunda is a wholesale Cuban bread supplier for restaurants, hotels, schools, foodservice operators, and distributors. Frozen, thaw-and-serve product is available through 46 distributor locations — including Sysco, US Foods, PFG (Performance Food Group), GFS (Gordon Food Service), Ben E. Keith, Cheney Brothers, Shamrock Foods and Northern Haserot — reaching buyers in 35+ states; fresh-daily delivery in the Tampa Bay area.

It comes from a family that's been doing the same thing, the same way, since 1915.

05FAQ

Wholesale Cuban Bread, answered.

Common questions from foodservice operators, distributors, and wholesale buyers.

Do you sell Cuban bread wholesale?

+

Yes. La Segunda supplies Cuban bread and related bakery products to restaurants, hotels, schools, foodservice operators, multi-unit groups, and distributors.

Where is La Segunda Cuban bread available?

+

La Segunda frozen bread products are available through foodservice distribution in multiple states, with fresh local delivery available in the Tampa Bay area.

What wholesale products are available?

+

Wholesale products include Cuban bread, honey wheat Cuban bread, media noche, coca buns, hoagie rolls, bucket bread, and basket bread. See the full product lineup.

Who should contact La Segunda for wholesale pricing?

+

Restaurants, hotels, schools, foodservice operators, distributors, and multi-location groups can contact Giuseppe for wholesale pricing, availability, and distribution questions. Start a wholesale inquiry.

Does La Segunda work with distributors?

+

Yes. La Segunda works with foodservice distribution partners to make frozen Cuban bread products available outside the Tampa Bay area. See the distribution network.

What makes La Segunda Cuban bread different?

+

La Segunda has baked Cuban bread in Ybor City since 1915, using a tradition defined by a long loaf, light interior, crisp crust, and signature palmetto-leaf split.
